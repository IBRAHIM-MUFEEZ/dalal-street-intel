DCF VALUATION ENGINE
// THREE-STAGE DCF · CAPM COST OF EQUITY · WACC · GORDON GROWTH TERMINAL VALUE
CAPM — Cost of Equity
Formula
Ke = Rf + β × (Rm − Rf)
Rf = Risk-free rate, β = Beta, (Rm−Rf) = Equity Risk Premium
—
WACC — Weighted Avg Cost of Capital
Formula
WACC = (E/V)·Ke + (D/V)·Kd·(1−T)
Three-Stage Growth
Formula
PV = Σ FCFEt/(1+WACC)^t + TV/(1+WACC)^n
TV = FCFEn·(1+g∞) / (WACC − g∞)
